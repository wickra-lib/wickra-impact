# Roadmap

Wickra Impact is pre-1.0; the surface may change before the first release.

## Now (0.1.x)

- The order-book-walk fill engine over the inherited `wickra-backtest` types.
- The `command_json` boundary and the reference CLI.
- The ten language bindings, the golden corpus and the full CI matrix.

## Next

- Additional impact models beyond the level-walk (square-root / power-law
  market-impact curves).
- Per-order impact decay and temporary-vs-permanent impact split.
- Partial-fill and time-in-force handling against the recorded book.

## Later

- A first tagged release published across the language registries (USER-GO).

Nothing here is a commitment; priorities follow real use.
